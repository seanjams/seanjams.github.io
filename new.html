Key Wheel

Demo Repo

The Key Wheel is a network of 7-tone musical scales. I discovered this layout during mathematical research at UC Davis. Some simple rules are used to determine which notes are able to be adjusted, and the resulting visualization displays the sonic distance between all Diatonic (Major, Ionian), Melodic Minor (Altered), and Wholetone (Neapolitan) scales. Use the selector to see where certain chords/scales live and hear the notes.
